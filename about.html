About IntelliMake

Research Hub & Factory initiative at Wayne State University

IntelliMake links campus research with hands-on factory learning. We prototype autonomous production lines with AI agents, robotics, and digital twins while keeping operators in the loop.

The hub connects faculty, students, and industry partners so ideas move quickly from lab to line.
