About IntelliMake

Research Hub & Factory initiative at Wayne State University

IntelliMake is a living lab that links campus research with hands-on factory learning. We prototype autonomous, people-in-the-loop production lines powered by AI agents, robotics, IoT, and digital twins.

What drives us

Uniting faculty, students, and industry to turn bold ideas into deployable automation.

How we work

Human-centered orchestration, rapid prototyping, and closed-loop learning on the factory floor.
