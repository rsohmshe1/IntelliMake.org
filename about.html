About IntelliMake

Research Hub & Factory at Wayne State University College of Engineering

IntelliMake is a campus-born initiative where faculty, students, and industry partners co-create autonomous, people-in-the-loop production lines powered by AI agents, robotics, IoT, and digital twins.
